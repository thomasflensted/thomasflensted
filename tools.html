Thomas Flensted Tools
During my time as a filmmaker and motion designer, I've developed a range of tools (mainly for After Effects) to ease my workflow. They might be helpful for you too, so feel free to download/buy any of them below.

PathTrimmer

Add and keyframe trim paths with a single click on all selected layers.

EasyMatte

Quickly generate perfectly fitting alpha mattes for all of your selected layers.

Delayer

Make selected layers follow another layer with a single click and control delay easily.

BoxedIn

Generate dynamic textboxes with easy control of padding, color and text

NumSequence

Generate a sequence of numbers with handy controls. Ideal for timelines.

CleverCounter

Counter with many formatting options such as unit and thousands separation.

AnchorControl

Control where to lock your anchor point with a dropdown menu.

RenameLayers

Rename and enumerate selected layers instantly.
