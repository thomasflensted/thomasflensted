Thomas Flensted Tools
During my time as a filmmaker and motion designer, I've developed a range of tools (mainly for After Effects) to streamline and ease my workflow. They might be helpful for you too, so feel free to download/buy/use any of them below.

EasyMatte

Quickly generate perfectly fitting alpha mattes for all of your selected layers.

SRT Formatter

Remove whitespace, capitalize letters, prepend and append subtitles with dashes, and more.

PathTrimmer

Trim paths on selected layers. Choose either trim in, trim out or trim in and out

Easy TextBox

Generate dynamic textbox for selected layers with easy control of padding, color, etc.

NumSequence

Generate a sequence of numbers that starts and ends where you choose to. Ideal for timelines.

CleverCounter

Simple counter with options to include unit, thousands separator, and more.

AnchorControl

Control where to lock your anchor point with a dropdown menu.

RenameLayers

Rename and enumerate selected layers instantly.
